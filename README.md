1.  Business Overview

B’s Therapy is a beauty and wellness business focused on providing professional eyelash extension services. The brand is built around enhancing natural beauty, boosting confidence, and offering clients a relaxing, luxury beauty experience.

The business targets clients who want high-quality lash treatments in a calm and stylish environment.

2.  Business Description

B’s Therapy specialises in lash artistry services such as:

Classic lash sets
Hybrid lash sets
Volume lash sets
Lash refills and maintenance

The business focuses on precision, comfort, and customer satisfaction. Each treatment is designed to improve the client’s appearance while maintaining a natural and elegant look.

3.  Business Objectives
Provide high-quality eyelash extension services
Build a strong beauty brand identity
Attract and retain loyal clients
Offer affordable yet professional beauty services
Create an easy online booking/contact experience

The B’s Therapy website was created to:
